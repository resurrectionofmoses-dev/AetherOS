### Step 2: Resource Transformation (Value Addition)

```typescript
THE SECOND WAY MONEY INCREASES

Example: Smelting Steel
=======================

Agent: Factory Worker
Action: Turn iron ore into steel

Inputs:
  • 500 kg iron ore = 1,000 CPH
  • 100 kWh electricity = 100 CPH
  • 10 hours labor = 200 CPH
  Total input cost: 1,300 CPH

Output:
  • 400 kg steel
  • Market rate: 5 CPH per kg
  • Total value: 400 × 5 = 2,000 CPH

Why is this valid?
  • Steel is MORE useful than iron ore
  • Transformation required skill and energy
  • Market values this higher
  • Value was ADDED, not created from nothing

Reserve balance:
  Before: 1,000 CPH (iron ore)
  After: 1,700 CPH (consumed ore, created steel, net +700)
```
